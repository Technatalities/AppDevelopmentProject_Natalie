About Us

Fighting middle man prices like how we fight climate change.

What Do We Do?

Supply Walrus is dedicated to connecting suppliers and consumers directly. Cutting down and eliminating the price that stores mark up.

Here at Supply Walrus, we want to ensure that consumers can avoid paying extra for stores that act as the middle man. By connecting you directly to teh suppliers. You, the consumer, can save money and avoid exorbitant store prices.

Our Values:

We believe in Fair Prices for All and that all things should remain afforable and easily accessibe to everyone, without people having to worry about added on store markups.

Like Climate Change and their threat against Walrus', stores marking up prices can be a threat to our shopping experience. Supply Walrus would like to fix this, one step at a time One consumer at a time. One partnered Supplier at a time.

Our Projects:

At Supply Walrus, we believe in helping our earth while we combat store markup prices. Did you know stores markup an average of 50%?!

20% of what we make, connecting you to our partnered suppliers, will go to a credible charity to fight climate change. Charities include Cool Earth and Earth Justice
